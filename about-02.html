About Us



Established in 1956, the Tamil Nadu Motor Parts Dealer Association (TNMPDA) has grown into a powerful network of automobile part dealers across Tamil Nadu. With over six decades of dedication and commitment, we continue to support and uplift our members by fostering collaboration and excellence in the automotive industry.

Empowering Dealers, Enriching Lives

Our Mission

At TNMPDA, our mission is to empower motor parts dealers through advocacy, education, and collaboration. We strive to enhance the quality of life for our members by providing resources, support, and opportunities for growth, ensuring the success of our community and the industry as a whole.
