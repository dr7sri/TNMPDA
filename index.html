About TNMPDA



THE TAMILNAD MOTOR PARTS DEALERS' ASSOCIATION

Established in 1956, the Tamil Nadu Motor Parts Dealer Association (TNMPDA) has grown into a powerful network of automobile part dealers across Tamil Nadu.

With over six decades of dedication and commitment, we continue to support and uplift our members by fostering collaboration and excellence in the automotive industry.

Our Achievements

Celebrating 60 Years of Excellence

Over the past six decades, TNMPDA has built a legacy of achievements. From advocating for policy changes to organizing large-scale expos, we have consistently championed the interests of our members and the automotive industry. Our milestones include:

  • Advocacy: Successfully lobbying for beneficial regulations and policies.
  • Education: Hosting workshops and seminars to keep our members informed.
  • Networking: Building strong connections within the industry and beyond.
Expos and Events

Showcasing Innovation and Dedication

Every four to five years, TNMPDA hosts a grand expo, showcasing the latest innovations and celebrating the dedication of our members. These events are a platform to:

  • Demonstrate Excellence: Highlight the skills and achievements of our members.
  • Engage the Public: Educate the community about the automotive industry.
  • Inspire the Next Generation: Encourage young enthusiasts to join our field.
Monthly Meetings

Fostering Connection and Growth

TNMPDA holds regular monthly meetings to foster connection and growth among our members. These gatherings provide an opportunity to:

  • Share Knowledge: Exchange ideas and insights with industry experts.
  • Celebrate Success: Recognize and reward the hard work of our members.
  • Strengthen Bonds: Build lasting relationships within our community
